Welcome to TIKUJI-NI-WADI Fun Resort

So close to the city, yet so near to nature

Founded and established by Late Shri Pravinchandra A. Parekh over 30 years ago and approved and recognized as a Tourist Attraction by the Tourism Department, Government of Maharashtra,now, we want you to enjoy every moment here, to the fullest. Water Park, Amusement Park, Go-Karts, Kids Land, Dinosaur Park, Aquarium and many other attractions… We have it all. Right here, at Tikuji-Ni-Wadi Visamo.

Tikuji-Ni-Wadi - The Family Fun Resort, resting between the beautiful hills and lush green valleys of ManpadaChitalsar, Thane, 40 Kilometres away from the hustle and bustle of Metropolis called Mumbai, a dream of cloud of beauty (natural and man made) and incredible entertainment.

And speaking of excitement, every second out here is filled with it. Our new rides, the first of its kind in India have to be experienced to be believed. Be it the adventure of zooming amidst treetops in our rotating spin coaster or the thrill of the UFO Cycle whizzing along a 500 meters track in mid-air. The experience of revolving 360 degrees in a space ship or be it the sheer thrill of challenging gravity by being a top the whirlwind chairs and children's delight the climbing car. Over 30 more exciting rides have it all. And when it comes to kids, our India's biggest play station is the ultimate activity with its vast scope and unlimited thrills for unlimited time. And it's perfectly complemented by wave pool, kids pool, lazy river and exciting 12 slides of pure adventure.

Tikuji-Ni-Wadi promises you the ride of your life and if you thought that's all, you are mistaken. Our lush green environs spread across 20 acres of vastness, lip-smacking delicacies, and our special thali, and now introduced multi cuisine food in four different locations, all hold you spellbound. Cosy home like cottages and the experience of relaxing on our Khatla beds beneath the serenity of trees... All speak about one thing - being here is bound to be an experience to remember.

Tikuji-Ni-Wadi has now become renowned as a favoured retreat for breath of fresh air and clean fun. A serene relaxation under the expanse of tranquil trees with village type cots, an experience one cannot forget. Ideal for the young and old.

NOTE: THE MANAGEMENT HAS THE RIGHT TO SHUTDOWN ANY RIDES AND AREAS OF ENTERTAINMENT DUE TO UNFORESEEN CIRCUMSTANCES AND WEATHER CONDITIONS.
